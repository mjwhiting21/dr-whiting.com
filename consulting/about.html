About

Academic depth, field awareness, and conservation judgment.

Dr. Jericho Whiting is listed by BYU-Idaho as a Biology faculty member. His public directory profile connects his work to wildlife conservation and biology research, making him a strong fit for projects that require ecological literacy and practical communication.

Consulting engagements can support agencies, landowners, nonprofits, developers, and education-focused organizations that need wildlife expertise presented in a clear, decision-ready format.

Working approach

Useful science is science that helps someone decide what to do next.

Evidence First

Recommendations begin with ecological context, available research, and the questions a project actually needs to answer.

Field-Aware

Good plans respect the real constraints of habitat, season, access, timing, and the species sharing the landscape.

Plainly Delivered

Findings can be organized for landowners, teams, agencies, and decision-makers without sacrificing scientific care.
